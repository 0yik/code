User friendly high quality Business Analytics Reports

Generate Very Attractive Business Analytics reports with ease.

This Module generates high quality PDF,XLS files and Chart & Graph reports with facility to customize design on the fly(background color,font color etc).

7 Custom Reports For PDF,XLS,Chart & Graph

  1. Sales Report
  2. Overdue Report
  3. Warehouse Report
  4. Profit Analysis Report
  5. Purchase Analysis Report
  6. Cash Ledger Report
  7. Stock Report

Sales Report

  • Sales Analysis Report gives details of product by customer and warehouse for all customer or any particuler customer only.
  • Available Report Types.
    • product by customer
    • product by warehouse


Overdue Report

  • Overdue Report gives details about overdue payment of sales,POS and total overdue payments.
  • Available Report Types.
    • sales overdue
    • pos overdue
    • total overdue payment

Warehouse Report

  • Warehouse Analysis Report gives details of product data by warehouse.
  • Available Report Types.
    • product data by warehouse (for particular warehouse or all warehouse)

Profit Analysis Report

  • Profit Analysis Report gives details of product's profit,margin,bill by customer and by product.
  • Available Report Types.
    • By product
    • By customer

Purchase Analysis Report

  • Purchase Analysis Report gives details of product by vendor and by product.
  • Available Report Types.
    • By product
    • By vendor

Cash Ledger Report

  • Cash Ledger Report gives details of customer payment and vendor payment which is paid by cash only.

    • Stock Report

    • Stock Analysis Report gives details of product stock data by customer and warehouses.
    • Available Report Types.
      • By warehouse(particular warehouse or all warehouse)
