Making Payslip without Any Troubles

According to the Employment Act of Singapore, salary refers to all remuneration including allowances payable to an employee with respect to work done under the contract of service. There is no minimum salary requirement to be met by an employer; it is generally based on the mutual agreement between the employer and the employee. Salary or Gross rate of Pay does not include the following

1.Additional payments such as (a)Overtime Payments (b) Bonus Payments (c) Annual Wage Supplements 2.Any reimbursement made for expenses incurred in the course of his/her employment 3.Productivity incentive payments; and Travelling, food or housing allowances.
