POS Membership Cards

Provide membership cards to your customers for availing discounts

Features
  • Seller can create various membership templates with some associated discounts.
  • Membership cards are created and assigned to the customers.
  • Customers can use their membership cards to avail discount on their orders.
  • Seller can apply membership card by scanning membership card barcode.
  • POS Session View
  • Backend View
